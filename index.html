10 Power Analysis — Voltage & Current ▾

Battery Current

Battery voltage = 3.7 V
Efficiency = 0.85

Ibatt = Pusb / (3.7 × 0.85)

Mode 1:0.77 A

Mode 2:2.96 A

Battery Runtime

Capacity = 0.5 Ah

t = capacity / current

Mode 1:≈ 39 min

Mode 2:≈ 10 min

Logging Overhead

ΔI = I₂ − I₁1.33 A

ΔP = P₂ − P₁6.86 W

Mode 2 consumes significantly more power due to SD card write operations.

Logging overhead reduces battery runtime from ~39 minutes down to ~10 minutes.
